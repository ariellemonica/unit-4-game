You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. And we're going by blackjack rules here - if you go over, you lose!

You don't get to know how much each crystal is worth before you start. Start clicking to find out! The value of the crystals changes each time a new game starts, just to keep things exciting.
